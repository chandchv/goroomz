## Marketing/Sales Agent Guide

### Overview

As a Marketing/Sales Agent, your primary responsibility is to onboard new property owners onto the GoRoomz platform. You'll manage leads, track communications, and earn commissions for successful onboardings.

### Getting Started

1. **Login**: Access the internal management portal at your assigned URL
2. **Dashboard**: Upon login, you'll see your Agent Dashboard with key metrics
3. **Navigation**: Use the sidebar to access different features

### Your Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- **Properties Onboarded**: Total properties you've successfully onboarded
- **Pending Leads**: Number of leads currently in your pipeline
- **Commission Earned**: Total commission earned this month
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads converted to properties
- **Lead Pipeline**: Visual representation of leads by status
- **Recent Activities**: Your latest actions and updates
- **Commission Summary**: Breakdown of earned, pending, and paid commissions

### Managing Leads

#### Creating a New Lead

1. Click **"Create Lead"** button on your dashboard
2. Fill in the property owner information:
   - Property owner name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Business name
   - Property type (Hotel or PG)
   - Location details (address, city, state)
   - Estimated number of rooms
   - Lead source (referral, cold call, website, etc.)
3. Add any initial notes
4. Click **"Create Lead"**

The lead will be automatically assigned to you and appear in your pipeline.

#### Working with Leads

**View Lead Details**:
- Click on any lead card in your pipeline
- View complete property owner information
- See communication history
- Check uploaded documents
- View current status

**Update Lead Status**:
1. Open the lead detail modal
2. Click **"Update Status"** dropdown
3. Select new status:
   - **Contacted**: Initial contact made
   - **In Progress**: Actively working on onboarding
   - **Pending Approval**: Submitted for manager review
   - **Approved**: Onboarding approved
   - **Rejected**: Onboarding rejected
   - **Lost**: Lead did not convert
4. Add notes about the status change
5. Click **"Save"**

**Add Communication**:
1. Open the lead detail modal
2. Click **"Add Communication"**
3. Select communication type (Call, Email, Meeting, Note)
4. Enter subject and details
5. Set scheduled/completed time
6. Click **"Save"**

All communications are logged and visible in chronological order.

#### Uploading Documents

1. Open the lead detail modal
2. Navigate to **"Documents"** tab
3. Click **"Upload Document"**
4. Select document type:
   - Business License
   - Property Photos
   - Owner ID
   - Tax Certificate
   - Other
5. Drag and drop file or click to browse
6. Click **"Upload"**

Required documents must be uploaded before submitting for approval.

#### Submitting for Approval

Once all information is complete and documents are uploaded:

1. Open the lead detail modal
2. Review all information for accuracy
3. Click **"Submit for Approval"**
4. Lead status changes to "Pending Approval"
5. Your Regional Manager will be notified

### Tracking Commissions

#### Viewing Commission Status

1. Navigate to **"Commissions"** from the sidebar
2. View commission breakdown:
   - **Earned**: Commissions from approved onboardings
   - **Pending Payment**: Awaiting payment processing
   - **Paid**: Already paid commissions

#### Commission Details

Click on any commission to view:
- Property name and details
- Onboarding date
- Commission amount and rate
- Payment status
- Expected payment date (for pending)
- Payment date and method (for paid)

### Performance Tracking

#### Viewing Your Targets

1. Navigate to **"My Targets"** from the sidebar
2. View current month/quarter targets:
   - Target properties to onboard
   - Actual properties onboarded
   - Progress percentage
   - Target revenue
   - Actual revenue

#### Performance History

View your historical performance:
- Properties onboarded per month
- Conversion rates over time
- Commission earnings trends
- Average onboarding time

### Best Practices

1. **Regular Follow-ups**: Contact leads within 24 hours of assignment
2. **Detailed Notes**: Document all communications thoroughly
3. **Complete Information**: Ensure all required fields are filled before submission
4. **Document Quality**: Upload clear, legible documents
5. **Status Updates**: Keep lead status current to reflect actual progress
6. **Response Time**: Respond to manager feedback promptly

### Common Tasks

**Daily**:
- Check dashboard for new lead assignments
- Follow up on pending leads
- Update lead statuses
- Log communications

**Weekly**:
- Review commission status
- Check progress against targets
- Follow up on rejected onboardings

**Monthly**:
- Review performance metrics
- Plan strategy for next month
- Verify commission payments

### Troubleshooting

**Lead Not Assigned**:
- Contact your Regional Manager
- Verify you're assigned to the correct territory

**Document Upload Failed**:
- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Verify file type (PDF, JPG, PNG only)
- Try again or contact support

**Commission Not Showing**:
- Verify lead status is "Approved"
- Check if property owner account is active
- Contact Platform Admin if issue persists

## Regional Manager Guide

### Overview

As a Regional Manager, you oversee a team of agents within your assigned territory. You're responsible for approving onboardings, managing territories, setting targets, and monitoring team performance.

### Getting Started

1. **Login**: Access the internal management portal
2. **Dashboard**: View your Regional Manager Dashboard
3. **Territory**: Familiarize yourself with your assigned territory

### Your Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- **Team Overview**: Number of agents and their key metrics
- **Regional Statistics**: Properties, occupancy, and revenue in your region
- **Pending Approvals**: Onboardings awaiting your review
- **Team Performance**: Agent comparison charts
- **Territory Map**: Visual representation of your coverage area

### Managing Your Team

#### Viewing Agent Performance

1. Navigate to **"Team Performance"** from the sidebar
2. View list of all agents in your territory
3. Click on any agent to see detailed metrics:
   - Properties onboarded
   - Conversion rate
   - Commission earned
   - Current pipeline
   - Target progress

#### Setting Agent Targets

1. Navigate to **"Team Performance"**
2. Click on an agent
3. Click **"Set Target"**
4. Enter target details:
   - Period (Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly)
   - Start and end dates
   - Target properties to onboard
   - Target revenue
5. Click **"Save Target"**

Agents can view their targets on their dashboard.

#### Assigning Agents to Territory

1. Navigate to **"Territory Management"**
2. Click **"Assign Agent"**
3. Select agent from dropdown
4. Confirm assignment

Leads in the territory will be automatically assigned to agents.

### Approving Onboardings

#### Reviewing Pending Approvals

1. Navigate to **"Pending Approvals"** from the sidebar
2. View list of onboardings awaiting review
3. Click on any onboarding to review details

#### Approval Process

1. Review property owner information
2. Verify business details
3. Check uploaded documents:
   - Business license validity
   - Property photos quality
   - Owner ID verification
   - Tax certificates
4. Review agent notes and communication history

**To Approve**:
1. Click **"Approve"** button
2. Add approval notes (optional)
3. Confirm approval
4. System will:
   - Create property owner account
   - Generate and send credentials via email
   - Create commission record for agent
   - Update lead status to "Approved"

**To Reject**:
1. Click **"Reject"** button
2. Enter rejection reason (required)
3. Specify what needs to be corrected
4. Confirm rejection
5. System will:
   - Return lead to agent
   - Notify agent of rejection
   - Update lead status to "Rejected"

Agent can make corrections and resubmit.

### Territory Management

#### Viewing Territory

1. Navigate to **"Territory Management"**
2. View territory details:
   - Name and description
   - Assigned agents
   - Coverage areas (cities, states)
   - Territory boundaries on map
   - Statistics (properties, occupancy, revenue)

#### Managing Territory Boundaries

1. Click **"Edit Territory"**
2. Update territory information:
   - Name and description
   - Add/remove cities
   - Add/remove states
   - Adjust boundaries on map (if applicable)
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

#### Territory Statistics

View comprehensive territory statistics:
- Total properties in territory
- Occupancy rates
- Monthly revenue
- Number of agents
- Leads in progress
- Conversion rates

Filter by date range to view historical trends.

### Generating Reports

#### Team Performance Report

1. Navigate to **"Reports"**
2. Select **"Team Performance"**
3. Choose date range
4. Select agents to include (or all)
5. Click **"Generate Report"**
6. View report online or export as PDF/CSV

Report includes:
- Agent comparison table
- Properties onboarded per agent
- Conversion rates
- Commission earned
- Performance trends

#### Regional Analytics Report

1. Navigate to **"Reports"**
2. Select **"Regional Analytics"**
3. Choose date range
4. Click **"Generate Report"**

Report includes:
- Regional property count
- Occupancy trends
- Revenue trends
- Growth rate
- City-wise breakdown

### Best Practices

1. **Timely Approvals**: Review onboardings within 24-48 hours
2. **Thorough Review**: Verify all documents carefully
3. **Clear Feedback**: Provide specific reasons for rejections
4. **Regular Check-ins**: Meet with agents weekly
5. **Fair Targets**: Set realistic, achievable targets
6. **Recognition**: Acknowledge top performers
7. **Support**: Help struggling agents improve

### Common Tasks

**Daily**:
- Review pending approvals
- Check team dashboard
- Respond to agent questions

**Weekly**:
- Review team performance
- Conduct agent meetings
- Analyze territory statistics

**Monthly**:
- Set/update agent targets
- Generate performance reports
- Review territory strategy

### Troubleshooting

**Cannot Approve Onboarding**:
- Verify all required documents are uploaded
- Check document review status
- Ensure all required fields are complete

**Agent Not Receiving Leads**:
- Verify agent is assigned to territory
- Check territory boundaries
- Verify agent is active

**Territory Statistics Incorrect**:
- Refresh dashboard
- Check date range filters
- Contact Platform Admin if issue persists

## Operations Manager Guide

### Overview

As an Operations Manager, you oversee platform-wide operations, manage support tickets, monitor property health, and ensure smooth platform functioning across all regions.

### Getting Started

1. **Login**: Access the internal management portal
2. **Dashboard**: View your Operations Manager Dashboard
3. **Familiarize**: Review platform health metrics

### Your Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- **Platform Health**: Total properties, bookings, occupancy rate
- **Support Tickets**: Open tickets by priority
- **Property Health Alerts**: Properties requiring attention
- **Platform Trends**: Booking and revenue trends
- **Critical Alerts**: Issues requiring immediate action

### Managing Support Tickets

#### Viewing Tickets

1. Navigate to **"Support Tickets"** from the sidebar
2. View tickets organized by status:
   - **New**: Just created, not yet assigned
   - **In Progress**: Being worked on
   - **Waiting Response**: Awaiting property owner response
   - **Resolved**: Issue resolved, awaiting closure
   - **Closed**: Ticket closed

Filter by:
- Priority (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
- Category (Technical, Billing, Operations, Feature Request)
- Property owner
- Assigned staff member

#### Creating a Ticket

1. Click **"Create Ticket"**
2. Enter ticket details:
   - Property owner (search and select)
   - Property (if applicable)
   - Title
   - Description
   - Category
   - Priority
3. Assign to operations staff member
4. Click **"Create Ticket"**

#### Working with Tickets

**View Ticket Details**:
- Click on any ticket
- View complete ticket information
- See response timeline
- Check property owner details

**Assign Ticket**:
1. Open ticket details
2. Click **"Assign"**
3. Select staff member
4. Confirm assignment

**Update Status**:
1. Open ticket details
2. Click **"Update Status"**
3. Select new status
4. Add notes about the update
5. Click **"Save"**

**Add Response**:
1. Open ticket details
2. Click **"Add Response"**
3. Enter your message
4. Choose if response is internal (staff only) or customer-facing
5. Attach files if needed
6. Click **"Send Response"**

Customer-facing responses are sent to property owner via email.

**Resolve Ticket**:
1. Open ticket details
2. Click **"Resolve"**
3. Enter resolution summary
4. Click **"Confirm"**
5. Property owner is notified

**Close Ticket**:
1. Open resolved ticket
2. Click **"Close Ticket"**
3. Confirm closure

#### Ticket Best Practices

1. **Response Time**: Respond to high-priority tickets within 2 hours
2. **Clear Communication**: Provide clear, actionable responses
3. **Follow-up**: Check back on waiting tickets regularly
4. **Documentation**: Document all troubleshooting steps
5. **Escalation**: Escalate complex issues to Platform Admin

### Accessing Property Management

#### Searching for Properties

1. Use the global search bar in the header
2. Enter property name, owner name, email, or location
3. Select property from results

#### Accessing Property Interface

1. Search for and select a property
2. Click **"Access Property"**
3. You'll be taken to the property's internal management interface
4. You have full read and write access to:
   - Room management
   - Booking management
   - Payment management
   - Housekeeping and maintenance
   - Reports

All your actions are logged in the audit trail.

#### Exiting Property Interface

Click **"Return to Operations Dashboard"** to return to your dashboard.

### Monitoring Platform Health

#### Platform Health Metrics

Navigate to **"Platform Health"** to view:
- **API Response Times**: Average response time for API calls
- **Error Rates**: Percentage of failed requests
- **System Uptime**: Platform availability percentage
- **Active Connections**: Current active user sessions

#### Capacity Metrics

View platform capacity:
- Total properties
- Total rooms
- Current booking load
- Capacity utilization percentage

#### Activity Metrics

Monitor user activity:
- Active users (property owners and internal staff)
- Concurrent sessions
- Peak usage times
- Daily active users

### Managing Alerts

#### Viewing Alerts

Navigate to **"Alerts"** to view:
- **Zero Occupancy Alerts**: Properties with no bookings for 7+ days
- **Payment Failure Alerts**: Properties with payment processing issues
- **High-Priority Tickets**: Urgent support tickets
- **System Errors**: Platform errors affecting multiple properties

#### Responding to Alerts

1. Click on any alert
2. View alert details and affected properties
3. Take appropriate action:
   - Contact property owner
   - Create support ticket
   - Escalate to Platform Admin
4. Mark alert as resolved when addressed

### Broadcasting Announcements

#### Creating Announcements

1. Navigate to **"Announcements"**
2. Click **"Create Announcement"**
3. Enter announcement details:
   - Title
   - Content
   - Target audience:
     - All property owners
     - Specific region
     - Specific property type
   - Delivery method (Email, In-app, SMS)
   - Schedule (Send now or schedule for later)
4. Click **"Create Announcement"**

#### Viewing Announcement Statistics

1. Navigate to **"Announcements"**
2. Click on any sent announcement
3. View statistics:
   - Total recipients
   - Read count and percentage
   - Delivery status by channel
   - Failed deliveries

### Platform Analytics

Navigate to **"Platform Analytics"** to view:
- Booking trends over time
- Revenue trends
- Occupancy heatmap
- Regional comparison
- Property type breakdown
- Seasonal patterns

Export analytics reports as PDF or CSV.

### Best Practices

1. **Proactive Monitoring**: Check platform health daily
2. **Quick Response**: Address high-priority tickets immediately
3. **Property Support**: Provide excellent support to property owners
4. **Documentation**: Document all major issues and resolutions
5. **Communication**: Keep property owners informed of platform updates
6. **Escalation**: Know when to escalate to Platform Admin

### Common Tasks

**Daily**:
- Review platform health dashboard
- Check new support tickets
- Monitor critical alerts
- Respond to urgent issues

**Weekly**:
- Review ticket resolution metrics
- Analyze platform trends
- Check property health alerts
- Plan announcements

**Monthly**:
- Generate platform analytics reports
- Review support ticket trends
- Analyze platform capacity
- Plan improvements

### Troubleshooting

**Cannot Access Property**:
- Verify property exists in system
- Check your permissions
- Contact Platform Admin if issue persists

**Announcement Not Sending**:
- Verify target audience is selected
- Check scheduled time
- Verify email service is configured
- Contact Platform Admin

**Platform Health Metrics Not Loading**:
- Refresh dashboard
- Check your internet connection
- Contact Platform Admin if issue persists

## Platform Administrator Guide

### Overview

As a Platform Administrator, you manage internal users, configure system settings, manage subscriptions, and oversee API integrations. You have elevated permissions to configure the platform.

### Getting Started

1. **Login**: Access the internal management portal
2. **Dashboard**: View your Platform Admin Dashboard
3. **Review**: Familiarize yourself with system configuration

### Your Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- **User Management**: Total internal users and property owners
- **System Configuration**: Current system settings
- **Subscription Management**: Active subscriptions and renewals
- **API Usage**: Integration statistics
- **Notification Settings**: Alert configurations

### Managing Internal Users

#### Viewing Internal Users

1. Navigate to **"Internal Users"** from the sidebar
2. View list of all internal staff:
   - Agents
   - Regional Managers
   - Operations Managers
   - Platform Admins
   - Superusers

Filter by:
- Role
- Active status
- Territory
- Last login date

#### Creating Internal Users

1. Click **"Create User"**
2. Enter user details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Role (Agent, Regional Manager, Operations Manager, Platform Admin)
   - Territory (for Agents)
   - Manager (for Agents - select Regional Manager)
   - Commission Rate (for Agents)
3. Choose credential delivery:
   - **Send via email**: System generates password and emails user
   - **Display password**: Show temporary password to copy manually
4. Click **"Create User"**

User receives welcome email with login credentials.

#### Editing Internal Users

1. Click on any user
2. Click **"Edit User"**
3. Update user information:
   - Name, phone
   - Territory assignment
   - Commission rate
   - Active status
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

#### Deactivating Users

1. Click on user
2. Click **"Deactivate User"**
3. Confirm deactivation

Deactivated users:
- Cannot log in
- Historical data is preserved
- Can be reactivated later

#### Managing User Permissions

1. Click on user
2. Navigate to **"Permissions"** tab
3. Toggle individual permissions:
   - Can onboard properties
   - Can approve onboardings
   - Can manage agents
   - Can access all properties
   - Can manage system settings
   - Can view audit logs
   - Can manage commissions
   - Can manage territories
   - Can manage tickets
   - Can broadcast announcements
4. Click **"Save Permissions"**

### Configuring System Settings

#### Commission Rate Configuration

1. Navigate to **"System Configuration"**
2. Click **"Commission Rates"**
3. Configure rates:
   - **Default Rate**: Standard commission percentage
   - **Premium Rate**: Higher rate for premium properties
   - **Custom Rates**: Set specific rates for individual agents
4. Click **"Save Configuration"**

Changes apply to future onboardings only.

#### Regional Settings

1. Navigate to **"System Configuration"**
2. Click **"Regional Settings"**
3. Configure regions:
   - Create new regions
   - Define geographic boundaries
   - Assign Regional Managers
   - Set region-specific policies
4. Click **"Save Configuration"**

#### Platform Policies

1. Navigate to **"System Configuration"**
2. Click **"Platform Policies"**
3. Configure policies:
   - Payment terms
   - Cancellation policies
   - Service fees
   - Refund policies
4. Click **"Save Configuration"**

### Managing Subscriptions

#### Viewing Subscriptions

1. Navigate to **"Subscriptions"** from the sidebar
2. View all property owner subscriptions
3. Filter by:
   - Status (Active, Expired, Cancelled)
   - Plan type
   - Expiration date

#### Viewing Subscription Details

Click on any subscription to view:
- Property owner information
- Current plan and features
- Billing cycle
- Amount
- Start and end dates
- Auto-renewal status
- Payment history

#### Upgrading Subscriptions

1. Open subscription details
2. Click **"Upgrade Subscription"**
3. Select new plan
4. Choose billing cycle
5. Review prorated charges
6. Click **"Confirm Upgrade"**

System calculates prorated charges automatically.

#### Applying Discounts

1. Open subscription details
2. Click **"Apply Discount"**
3. Enter discount details:
   - Type (Percentage or Fixed Amount)
   - Value
   - Expiration date
4. Click **"Apply Discount"**

Discount is applied to next billing cycle.

#### Viewing Billing History

1. Open subscription details
2. Navigate to **"Billing History"** tab
3. View all invoices and payments:
   - Invoice number
   - Amount
   - Status (Paid, Pending, Failed)
   - Billing date
   - Payment method
   - Transaction ID

### Managing API Keys

#### Creating API Keys

1. Navigate to **"API Management"**
2. Click **"Create API Key"**
3. Enter API key details:
   - Name/Description
   - Permissions (select which endpoints can be accessed)
   - Expiration date
4. Click **"Create API Key"**
5. Copy the generated API key (shown only once)

#### Viewing API Keys

View list of all API keys:
- Name
- Permissions
- Active status
- Last used date
- Expiration date

#### Revoking API Keys

1. Click on API key
2. Click **"Revoke API Key"**
3. Confirm revocation

Revoked keys are immediately blocked from making requests.

#### Viewing API Usage

1. Click on API key
2. Navigate to **"Usage Statistics"** tab
3. View usage metrics:
   - Total requests
   - Successful requests
   - Failed requests
   - Average response time
   - Requests by endpoint
   - Requests by day

### Configuring Notifications

#### Notification Settings

1. Navigate to **"Notification Settings"**
2. Configure notification channels:
   - Email settings (SMTP configuration)
   - SMS settings (provider configuration)
   - In-app notification settings
3. Click **"Save Settings"**

#### Notification Rules

1. Navigate to **"Notification Rules"**
2. Configure rules for automatic notifications:
   - Lead assignment notifications
   - Approval notifications
   - Ticket notifications
   - Alert notifications
   - Reminder notifications
3. Set conditions and recipients for each rule
4. Click **"Save Rules"**

### Viewing Audit Logs

1. Navigate to **"Audit Logs"**
2. View all internal user actions
3. Filter by:
   - User
   - Action type
   - Resource type
   - Date range
   - Critical actions only
4. Click on any log entry to view details:
   - User who performed action
   - Action type
   - Resource affected
   - Changes made (before/after)
   - IP address
   - Timestamp

Export audit logs as CSV for compliance.

### Best Practices

1. **User Management**: Review user access regularly
2. **Security**: Rotate API keys periodically
3. **Monitoring**: Check API usage for anomalies
4. **Subscriptions**: Monitor expiring subscriptions
5. **Configuration**: Document all configuration changes
6. **Audit**: Review audit logs weekly for suspicious activity

### Common Tasks

**Daily**:
- Check new user requests
- Monitor API usage
- Review critical audit logs

**Weekly**:
- Review subscription renewals
- Check system configuration
- Analyze notification delivery

**Monthly**:
- Audit user permissions
- Review API key usage
- Generate compliance reports
- Update system policies

### Troubleshooting

**User Cannot Login**:
- Verify user is active
- Check email is correct

- Verify role permissions

- Check expiration date

## Security Best Practices

1. **Password Security**:
   - Use strong, unique passwords
   - Change password every 90 days
   - Never share your password

2. **Session Security**:
   - Always log out when finished
   - Don't leave sessions unattended
   - Use secure networks (avoid public WiFi)

3. **Data Protection**:
   - Don't share sensitive information via email
   - Verify recipient before sharing data
   - Follow data protection policies

4. **Device Security**:
   - Keep your device secure with password/PIN
   - Install security updates promptly
   - Use antivirus software

5. **Suspicious Activity**:
   - Report any suspicious activity immediately
   - Don't click on suspicious links
   - Verify requests for sensitive information

## Glossary

- **Lead**: Potential property owner being onboarded
- **Onboarding**: Process of registering a new property owner
- **Commission**: Payment earned by agents for successful onboardings
- **Territory**: Geographic area assigned to agents
- **Target**: Performance goal set for agents
- **Property Owner**: External user who owns and manages properties
- **Internal User**: Platform employee (agent, manager, admin, etc.)
- **Audit Log**: Record of all user actions in the system

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

**Q: Can I access the platform on mobile?**
A: Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: How often are commissions paid?**
A: Commission payment schedules are set by your organization. Check with your Regional Manager or Platform Admin.

**Q: What if I can't find a property in search?**
A: Verify the property is onboarded and active. Contact Operations Manager if you need access.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use the "Report Issue" feature in the help menu or contact support directly.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, most views have an "Export" button to download data as PDF or CSV.
